About

Social networks today allows us to express ourselves using short videos, text, and photos. Music is an extremely personal and important aspect of everyone’s lives, but yet there is no good way to share it digitally. NoiseHub is a social network for music, focusing on the connection people have with their music.

Features


Feed

We encourage communication by allowing you to choose any song from your Apple Music library, decide the twenty seconds that resonates most to you, add a message, and share it with the world. The feed is where you get to see all of this take place.


Jukebox

We firmly believe that music reflects your local culture, and we believe this is an important part of making music social. That is why we created what we call, "Jukebox." It allows you to see what songs other users near you are posting. The more people you get to download NoiseHub and post, the more people you’ll see.
